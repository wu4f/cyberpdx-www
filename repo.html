Teacher Lesson Plan Repository

After camp, teachers work on a lesson plan derived from camp ideas and activities and then come back to a Fall workshop to share materials they have created (reflections, course proposals, lesson plans, instructional modules, assessments). These lesson plans are intended to enable teaching of cyber in high schools.
